    Bir Billing

    “Paragliding in Bir Billing, Himachal Pradesh”
    Bir Billing is a Paragliding destination in the heart of Himalaya’s near famous tourist destination Dharamshala in Himachal Pradesh.
    Bir Billing Hosted Paragliding World cup in 2015. This was “First Paragliding World Cup of India.”

    HOW TO
    Reach?
    One can reach Bir Billing
    through Aeroplanes, trains, buses or taxi.
    Plane
    Kangra airport is nearest to the Bir. It is 67.5 km from there.
    Bus
    From Delhi, it takes around fourteen hours to reach Bir. Take the bus going to Pathankot, Baijnath, Manali, Shimla or Dharamshala.
    Train
    The nearest station is in Pathankot which is 112 km from Bir. A toy train connects From Pathankot to Ahju.
    Taxi
    It is the best and convenient way to travel to Bir. From Pathankot it takes 4-5 hours, from Dharmshala it takes 2 hours,
